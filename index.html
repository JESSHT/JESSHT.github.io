01 Data Analysis · SQL · Healthcare

Medical Insurance Health Plan Strategy 2026

Problem

Health plan selection for diabetic and hypertensive populations involved comparing plans across 5 regions with no normalized cost or risk framework, leaving decision-makers without reliable data.

Action

Built a 6-step Google BigQuery SQL pipeline applying risk-score normalization for fair cross-plan comparison. Automated reporting with a VBA-driven Access database and self-updating Excel dashboard with region filtering and printable outputs.

Impact

Surfaced Silver-tier plans as the consistent top performer across all conditions and geographies, giving stakeholders a data-backed, defensible framework for 2026 plan strategy decisions.

BigQuery · SQL · Excel VBA · MS Access · Risk Normalization
